So with freshers "week" is about wrapped up its time to bring out the grand finale. Christ's Amateur Dramatic Society present the annual charity sketch show with brilliant sketches and stand up. Christ's and non-Christ's alike are welcome and its Just £2 entry. All money goes to good causes (ie. not the CADS drinks fund) A fantastic way to start your Friday night.

Its also a great chance to meet the committee (or stare at them and chicken out) for freshers or anyone else who wants to get involved. we will all be there and easy to see in our hoodies.
